# overview of week 4

This week's class focused on exploring the role of AI in design, from practical applications (local AI and cloud AI) to ethical considerations (data bias, data sovereignty), and transitioning from the experiments of the previous weeks to a more comprehensive data-driven project. Compared with the previous week's focus on technical and formal experiments, this week emphasized critical thinking and design intention, particularly regarding how AI shapes our understanding and translation of data.
